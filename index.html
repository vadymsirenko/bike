Description

Comfort and Safety from HIMO

The HIMO C26 electric bicycle will allow you to reach your workplace or school quickly and comfortably without relying on public transport. The bike features a sturdy design and an impressive range, making it a reliable vehicle for modern urban conditions. The lightweight and durable frame, 26-inch wheels, and 7-speed transmission system allow you to move confidently not only on paved roads but also on challenging off-road trails.

Sturdy and Lightweight Frame

The HIMO C26 frame is made from a durable and lightweight aluminum alloy, ensuring high reliability, stability in emergency situations, excellent maneuverability, and comfortable movement on any road.

Combination of Modes to Increase Range

The HIMO C26 electric bicycle has an extended range. Its 10000 mAh battery with an intelligent energy consumption optimization system allows for up to 60 km on a single charge in electric mode. When using physical power on certain sections, the range increases to 100 km. The maximum speed of the HIMO C26 is 25 km/h.

Reliable Transmission

The 7-speed transmission system provides smooth and precise gear shifting, convenient speed control, and reliability in operation. By shifting gears, you can climb hills with minimal effort and safely descend.

All Information on the LCD Display

All information on the LCD display. The large informative display mounted on the handlebar shows all important information about the HIMO C26's operation in real time. You can find out your current speed, battery level, or distance traveled. The display is protected from external factors according to the IPX7 standard and is resistant to rain and dust.

Comfortable Saddle

The HIMO C26 electric bicycle is equipped with a bicycle saddle from the renowned brand Selle Royal. The three-dimensional gel structure of the saddle ensures optimal elasticity and high comfort while riding.
